ZENSUS 2011

This dataset contains disaggregated data concerning a building stock analysis for Germany, information about the occupancy of the buildings and socio/economic related data. Information concerning the type of heating systems used are reported too. The goal of the 2011 Census is to provide the most accurate snapshot possible of basic data on the countries population and the employment and housing conditions.
