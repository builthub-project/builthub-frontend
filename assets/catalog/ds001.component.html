Horizon 2020 HotMaps project: Building stock analysis

Complete building stock analysis for the EU27+UK. Values related to final energy consumption and useful energy demand for space heating, space cooling and domestic hot water, construction materials and methodologies, technologies used and building stock data/information (thermal transmittancy, building stock vintages and characteristics, household occupancy related data, etc.) can be found both for the residential and the non-residential sectors per building types and construction vintages.
